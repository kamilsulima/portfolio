About

Kamil comes from Zamość but currently lives between Tricity and Copenhagen. He is self-taught Front-end programmer and educated in Denmark multimedia designer. In spare time absorbed by reading books and writing short-stories which is closely related to the penchant for tapping the lines of code. He is meticulous and sensitive to aesthetic. At the same time, he always tries to find innovative and fresh solutions, preferably open source. He like to gain new experiences and deepen his knowledge. A man of few words, but patient listener and good companion. He has one year old son Franio and a great wife Monika.
